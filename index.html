About Us

VPN Owl keeps your online activity safe, secure, and private - for your eyes only! Trusted by 80+ million users worldwide for VPN stability, speed, and security.

With VPN Owl, feel confident that your internet activity (home, public wifi, cellular) is protected, and anonymous. It guards your passwords, credit card, and bank accounts details (amongst others) from hackers and trackers. Plus, gives you unrestricted access to global internet content. Along with that, it lets you unblock popular apps like WhatsApp, TikTok, WeChat, Telegram, Roblox, and lots more.
